3. My SQL queries were quite simple, as I was fetching the required information about steelers picks. I exported a csv called "Steeler Picks" with the columns Player, NFL_Team, Round, Ovr_Pick_No, Position, College, and Notes.

Sample:

SELECT Player, NFL_Team, Round, Ovr_Pick_No, Position, College, Notes
  FROM draft
  WHERE NFL_Team IN ('Pittsburgh Steelers')
  ORDER BY Round asc;
